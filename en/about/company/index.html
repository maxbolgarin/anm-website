About the Company

ANM is a development and property management company that has been operating in St. Petersburg's commercial real estate market for over 20 years. Our mission is to create a comfortable environment for the business community through the redevelopment of industrial areas.

The company's flagship project is the development and management of a former industrial zone on Obukhovskoy Oborony Avenue. The redevelopment concept was designed by Horan Keoghan Ryan Ltd, a British architectural firm, envisioning the transformation of historic industrial buildings into modern business centers while preserving their unique character.

Today, class B and B+ business centers operate on the territory of Aleksandro-Nevskaya Manufaktura, providing tenants with every level of comfort: from modern engineering systems to developed infrastructure and a promenade area on the Neva River embankment. The management company handles all operational matters and ensures the most comfortable conditions for conducting business.

Company History

Key development milestones

1837

Factory founded

A calico-printing factory was founded on the current property — one of the largest textile production sites in St. Petersburg.

1855

Main building constructed

The building of the current Fidel Business Loft (Liter A, Bldg. 3) was built as part of the Pal textile factory facilities.

1976

Administrative building constructed

A six-story administrative building (Liter A, Bldg. 2) was erected, which later became the ANM Business Center.

2004

Company established

ZAO "Aleksandro-Nevskaya Manufaktura" was founded. The property complex on Pr. Obukhovskoy Oborony was acquired for territory redevelopment.

2006

Reconstruction begins

The decision was made to preserve the historic 1855 building. Together with Architectural Bureau INTERCOLUMNIUM, a business loft reconstruction project was developed.

2009

Fidel Business Loft opens

Reconstruction completed and the class B+ Fidel Business Loft was put into operation. Since opening, occupancy has been maintained at 100%.

2019

New redevelopment phase

Start of territory redevelopment work aimed at constructing new commercial real estate. Reconstruction and modernization of engineering systems.
