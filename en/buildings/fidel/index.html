Fidel in Detail

Architecture, engineering systems, service

Architecture & Design

Business loft with designer finishes: linoleum, painted walls (brick and drywall), pendant lighting, double-glazed windows. The unique character of the industrial building is preserved and enhanced with modern solutions.

Engineering Systems

Category II power supply reliability, centralized Ciat air conditioning, supply and exhaust ventilation, own gas boiler room, fire alarm system, 4 elevators, 6 telecom operators.

Service & Management

Professional management company with 20 years of experience. 24/7 access, maintenance service, cleaning, territory security.
